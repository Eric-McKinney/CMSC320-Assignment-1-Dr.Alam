Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: Git enables rolling back to previous versions of a file and making
experimental changes on a branch without affecting the main branch until the
changes are done. It also enables collaboration for open source projects and
the like.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be applied to almost any field. One example is
advertising since companies want to market their goods or services as
effectively as possible. Applying data science techniques can allow companies
to assess how effective certain ads are and improve their advertising and
consequently their profits.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging tries to apply the commits from one branch to another starting
from when they diverged. Rebasing simply moves a branch's starting point to the
head of another branch. Merging affects the branch being merged onto, but not
the branch being merged. Rebasing affects the branch being rebased, but not the
branch the first branch is being rebased onto.
